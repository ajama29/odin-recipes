Chicken Breast

Description

Wonderful rustic Italian style pasta dish! Meal can be dressed up or down with accompaniments to this tasty pasta.

  • 2 large bone-in chicken breast halves with skin
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Add Salt
  3. Step 3: Add the Pasta
  4. Step 4: Stir
  5. Step 5: Taste the Pasta
  6. Step 6: Drain
  7. Step 7: Removing Ravioli
  8. Step 8: Stir in the Sauce
  9. Step 9: Add Pasta Water
  10. Step 10: Buon Appetito!
